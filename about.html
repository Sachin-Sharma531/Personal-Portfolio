My Journey

I began my journey into web development via small-scale projects, including a timetable, a survey form, and a profile card. Each project taught me something new — layout positioning, content arrangement, or experimenting with color and typography. These smaller projects helped me realize how design and functionality are interdependent and continue to motivate me to create more user-friendly websites.
